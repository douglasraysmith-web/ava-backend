# AVA Owner Demo Ready Script v7.8.4

## Demo goal
Show that Ava is the AV-side intelligence for mtthorne.com, not a generic chatbot, while making clear which features are prepared, gated, or not live yet.

## Say this first
Ava is ready for owner-demo and deployment-channel testing. She is not fully live until the account-side proof checks pass.

## Demo prompts
1. Who are you?
2. What does AV mean on this site?
3. My TV has picture but no sound. What should I check first?
4. I want a home theater but do not know what I need.
5. Can you find device drivers and manuals?
6. Can you store customer information?
7. Can you control hardware?
8. What happens if ArchE is offline?
9. Continue your answer from where you stopped.
10. What do you need from me to give a proposal-ready AV plan?

## Expected behavior
Ava should answer directly, ask at most one useful clarifying question, keep owner/private/customer features gated, and clearly separate prepared capabilities from verified-live capabilities.
